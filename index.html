Knowledge Check

A pair of shoes costs ₱4,000. It is on sale for 20% off. What is the sale price?

20% of ₱4,000 is ₱800 (the discount). ₱4,000 - ₱800 = ₱3,200.

Knowledge Check

A real estate agent sells a house for ₱2,500,000 and earns a 3% commission. How much money did the agent make?

Commission = Price × Rate. 3% as a decimal is 0.03. ₱2,500,000 × 0.03 = ₱75,000.
